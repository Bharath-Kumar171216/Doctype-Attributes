YAMAHA R15S

The Yamaha R15S is a sporty and high-performance motorcycle, inspired by Yamaha’s racing DNA. It carries forward the legacy of the R-series with its sleek, aerodynamic design and sharp styling that appeals to those who crave speed and precision. Powered by a 155cc, liquid-cooled, fuel-injected engine with Variable Valve Actuation (VVA), the R15S delivers impressive performance, especially in the higher rev ranges, making it ideal for riders looking for excitement on the road. The engine is paired with a smooth 6-speed gearbox, enhancing its dynamic riding experience. What sets the R15S apart is its agility, thanks to its lightweight frame and advanced suspension system featuring telescopic forks in the front and a monoshock at the rear. The bike’s riding position is aggressive yet comfortable, with clip-on handlebars and rear-set footpegs providing a racing feel. Equipped with a dual-channel ABS for enhanced braking performance, the R15S ensures safety during high-speed rides. Its fully digital instrument cluster provides essential information at a glance, while the sharp LED headlamps and tail lights contribute to its sporty appeal. Overall, the Yamaha R15S is a perfect blend of performance, style, and advanced technology, making it an attractive option for young enthusiasts and experienced riders who seek a balance between everyday usability and track-inspired thrills.
